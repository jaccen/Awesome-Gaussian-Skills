### Paper Highlights

1. **GEMM-GS** (DAC 2026) — first method to exploit GPU Tensor Cores for 3DGS rendering via GEMM reformulation; 1.42x + additional 1.47x speedup
2. **EmoTaG** (CVPR 2026) — emotion-aware talking head on GS with FLAME parameter space reformulation; SOTA in emotional expressiveness
3. **E2EGS** (CVPR 2026) — fully pose-free 3D reconstruction from event cameras using edge-weighted Gaussian optimization
4. **Nighttime AD GS** (ICRA 2026) — physically-based rendering with BRDF for nighttime autonomous driving scene reconstruction
5. **HDR-NSFF** (ICLR 2026) — 4D HDR dynamic scene modeling from alternating-exposure video; first real-world HDR-GoPro dataset
6. **DiffSoup** — triangle soup with binary opacity as extreme simplification alternative to GS; orders-of-magnitude model reduction
7. **VIRGi** (TPAMI 2026) — instant recoloring of 3DGS scenes preserving view-dependent effects; 2-second propagation
8. **RDSplat** — first watermarking method resilient against diffusion model editing for 3DGS assets
