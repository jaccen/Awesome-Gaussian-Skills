### Paper Details

1. **MesonGS++** (Tsinghua / NTU Singapore)
   - Post-training 3DGS compression codec with automatic hyperparameter search
   - Joint importance-based pruning + octree geometry coding + selective VQ + mixed-precision quantization
   - Optimizes reserve ratio and bit-width via 0-1 integer linear programming under target budget
   - 34x compression while preserving fidelity; can surpass vanilla 3DGS PSNR at 20x

2. **Semantic Foam** (UBC / Google DeepMind)
   - CVPR 2026 Highlight — extends Radiant Foam for semantic scene decomposition
   - Volumetric Voronoi mesh with explicit semantic feature field at cell level
   - Outperforms Gaussian Grouping and SAGA on object-level segmentation
   - Direct spatial regularization improves cross-view consistency

3. **EnerGS** (ETH Zurich / Tsinghua)
   - Models partial geometric priors as continuous energy field
   - Soft energy guidance instead of hard constraints for 3DGS optimization
   - Effective for large-scale outdoor scenes with incomplete LiDAR coverage
   - Mitigates overfitting during training

4. **BiSplat-WRF** (TU Berlin)
   - Planar Gaussian Splatting adapted for wireless radiance field reconstruction
   - Bilinear Spatial Transformer captures long-range EM dependencies
   - Surpasses NeRF-based and prior GS-based baselines on SSIM

5. **IDESplat** (UESTC)
   - Iterative depth probability estimation for feed-forward 3DGS
   - Depth Probability Boosting Unit (DPBU) + Gaussian Focus Module (GFM)
   - Progressively refines depth via multi-warp epipolar attention
