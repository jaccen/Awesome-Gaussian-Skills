## New Papers (2)

### 1. FreeTimeGS++ (Dynamic Scene Methods)
- **ArXiv**: 2605.03337
- **Authors**: Lucas Yunkyu Lee, Soonho Kim, Youngwook Kim, Sangmin Kim, Jaesik Park (POSTECH)
- **Core**: Comprehensive analysis and principled improvement of 4D Gaussian Splatting
- **Key Innovation**: Emergent temporal partitioning via Gaussian durations; gated marginalization + neural velocity fields for stable dynamic representations
- **Category**: Dynamic Scene Methods

### 2. D-Rex (Human & Avatar / Material & Relighting)
- **ArXiv**: 2604.27871
- **Authors**: Timo Teufel, Xilong Zhou, Umar Iqbal, Jan Kautz, Marc Habermann, Vladislav Golyanik, Christian Theobalt (MPI Informatics / SAIF / NVIDIA)
- **Venue**: SIGGRAPH 2026
- **Core**: Diffusion rendering for relightable expressive avatars
- **Key Innovation**: Decouples relighting from avatar modeling; first method with strong facial animation + relighting; applicable to any white-light avatar system
- **Category**: Human & Avatar Methods (cross-listed with Material & Relighting)
