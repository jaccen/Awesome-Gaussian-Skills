### Paper Details

1. **Softmax-GS** (Oregon State / Google)
   - CVPR 2026 Findings — replaces standard alpha-compositing with learnable softmax-based competition
   - Continuous spectrum from smooth color blending to crisp boundaries
   - Preserves order invariance and transmittance consistency

2. **LeGS** (Harbin Institute of Technology / Alibaba)
   - CVPR 2026 Findings — paradigm shift: heuristic density control to fully learnable RL policy
   - Closed-form O(N) reward computation via sensitivity analysis
   - Outperforms heuristic methods on Mip-NeRF 360, Tanks & Temples, Deep Blending

3. **GETA-3DGS**
   - First end-to-end automatic joint structured pruning and quantization for 3DGS
   - 3DGS-aware QADG + render-aware saliency + heterogeneous mixed-precision
   - ~5x storage reduction, no per-scene thresholds

4. **2D-SuGaR** (TU Munich / Meta Reality Labs)
   - Enhances 2DGS with monocular depth and normal priors for robustness
   - Depth-guided Gaussian initialization + clustering-based degenerate pruning
   - SOTA mesh reconstruction on DTU while preserving NVS quality

5. **Structure-Aware Densification** (MPII / Meta)
   - SIGGRAPH 2026 — frequency-aware anisotropic splitting replacing heuristic densification
   - Multi-scale frequency analysis (structure tensors + Laplacian scale space)
   - Per-Gaussian per-axis violation metric; faster convergence + superior high-freq quality

6. **VkSplat**
   - Eurographics 2026 SP — first fully Vulkan-based 3DGS training pipeline
   - 3.3x speed + 33% VRAM reduction over CUDA+PyTorch; cross-vendor compatible

7. **GOR-IS** (Nanjing University)
   - 3D object removal in intrinsic space with physically consistent light transport
   - Handles non-Lambertian surfaces; +13% LPIPS, +2dB PSNR

8. **HumanSplatHMR** (University of Michigan)
   - Joint HMR pose refinement + high-fidelity avatar learning
   - Closes loop between pose estimation and differentiable rendering

9. **GSDrive** (TU Darmstadt)
   - 3DGS for differentiable RL reward shaping in E2E driving policy
   - Dense feedback instead of sparse catastrophic events

10. **GLMap** (CAS / ICT)
    - CVPR 2026 — multi-scale Gaussian-Language map for zero-shot embodied navigation
    - Gaussian Estimator analytically derives parameters without gradient optimization

11. **FieryGS** (Peking University / NVIDIA)
    - ICLR 2026 — physics-integrated fire synthesis within 3DGS pipeline
    - Supports flame propagation, smoke dispersion, surface carbonization

12. **SplAttN** (SWJTU)
    - ICML 2026 Spotlight — Gaussian soft splatting for point cloud completion
    - Identifies "Cross-Modal Entropy Collapse" failure mode

13. **Fake3DGS** (University of Modena)
    - ICPR 2026 — first benchmark for 3D manipulation detection in neural rendering

14. **SandSim** (East China Normal University)
    - Curve-guided Gaussian representation for sand painting process reconstruction

15. **RGS** (Xidian University)
    - Residual wavelet-GS for ultra sparse-view CBCT reconstruction
    - Spectrally-decoupled: geometric base + residual detail

16. **RESPIRE** (UNC Chapel Hill)
    - CT-informed mesh-anchored GS for dynamic bronchoscopy
    - Breathing phase as scalar; 1.22mm target localization; eliminates breath-hold

17. **Color-Encoded Illumination** (Weizmann Institute)
    - CVPR 2026 Highlight — color-coded light encodes temporal dimension
    - First high-speed volumetric reconstruction using only low-speed cameras

18. **HeroGS** (CAS/UCAS Vision Group)
    - CVPR 2026 — hierarchical guidance (image to region to pixel) for sparse-view 3DGS

19. **Sparse-View 3DGS Wild**
    - Diffusion-guided sparse-view enhancement for unconstrained scenes
    - PSNR +17.2%, SSIM +10.8%, LPIPS +4.0%

20. **3DGS AD Safety Eval** (Lund University / Volvo)
    - SafeComp 2026 — first industrial-fidelity 3DGS evaluation for AD
    - Analyzes vehicle/pedestrian reconstruction from novel viewpoints
